Our Story

Built on family.
Grown through partnership.

Sandalwood West was founded as a vehicle to grow a family business and to build partnerships with people who share our values. Over more than a decade, Ankit has sourced, structured, and operated the firm's acquisitions — from coastal California motels to industrial laundry operations in Calgary. The platform has grown to include partners with deep expertise across CPG, manufacturing, technology, and international trade.

Andrew and Ankit aligned naturally over years — sharing the same long-term goals before becoming business partners. The family office at the core of Sandalwood West is owned by Ankit and his cousin Jay. We are self-funded and have not accepted outside capital. Every deal has been built on our own conviction and values.
